Carrot Cake

Carrot cake is a moist, spiced cake made with grated carrots, which add natural sweetness and texture. It's often flavored with cinnamon, nutmeg, and vanilla, and commonly includes nuts or raisins. Topped with a rich cream cheese frosting, carrot cake is a classic dessert known for its balance of warmth and sweetness.

Ingredients

For Cake:

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1½ cups all purpose flour
  • 1 cup sugar (or brown sugar)
  • ½ cup vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • ½ baking soda
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • ½ nutmeg (Optional)
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ½ cup chopped walnuts or raisins (Optional)

Cream Cheese Frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ½ cup butter, softened
  • 1½ cups powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Preheat oven - Set oven to 350ºF (175ºC)and grease a cake pan.

  2. Mix dry Ingredients - In a bowl, whisk flour, baking powder, baking soda,cinnamon, nutmeg and salt.

  3. Mix wet Ingredients - In another bowl, beat eggs, sugar, oil, and vanilla until smooth.

  4. Combine & add carrots - Gradually mix dry ingredients into wet ingredients, then fold in grated carrots (and nuts/raisins if using).

  5. Bake - Pour butter into the pan and bake for 30-35 minutes or unless a toothpick comes out clean. Let cool.

  6. Prepare frosting - Beat cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar and vanilla until smooth.

  7. Frost and serve - Spread frosting over the cooled cake, slice and enjoy.
