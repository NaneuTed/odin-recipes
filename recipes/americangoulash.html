American Goulash

American goulash is a hearty, one-pot dish made with ground beef, elbow macaroni, and a savory tomato-based sauce. Seasoned with garlic, onions, and herbs, it's a simple, comforting meal that's easy to prepare and widely enjoyed at family gatherings.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 small onion diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (15 oz) tomato sauce
  • 1 can (15 oz) diced tomatoes
  • 1 cup beef broth or water
  • 1½ cups elbow macaroni
  • 1 tbsp Italian seasoning
  • ½ tbsp paprika(Optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • ½ cup shredded cheese(Optional,for topping)

Steps

  1. Cook the beef and onions:
    • In a large pot,brown the ground beef over medium heat.
    • Add diced onions and cook until soft.
    • Drain excess fat if needed.

  2. Add garlic and seasoning:
    • Stir in minced garlic, Italian seasoning, paprika, salt and pepper.
    • Cook for about 30 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Simmer the sauce:
    • Add tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, beef broth(or water).
    • Stir well and bring to a gentle simmer.

  4. Cook the pasta:
    • Stir in the uncooked elbow macaroni, cover, and let it cook for about 12-15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until pasta is tender.

  5. Finish and serve:
    • If desired stir in shredded cheese or sprinkle it on top.
    • Serve warm and enjoy.
